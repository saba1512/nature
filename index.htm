Tree

Trees are perennial woody plants that typically have a single main stem, or trunk, supporting branches and leaves

Flower

A flower is the reproductive structure of flowering plants, or angiosperms, which produce seeds.

Leaf

a flattened, green plant organ attached to a stem that performs photosynthesis, the process of converting sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide into food (sugar) and oxygen

Land

Land information includes records and systems related to the ownership, use, and characteristics of land.

Sky

The term "sky" can refer to the atmosphere as seen from Earth, including the blue daytime sky and the night sky with stars and planets, or more formally as the "celestial sphere" in astronomy.

Sunset

Sunset is the daily disappearance of the Sun below the horizon, caused by Earth's rotation.
